See the natural beauty and learn the local history of Harpswell Maine and the islands of Casco Bay via boat
Casco Bay has many historic lighthouses to visit, like Portland Head Light, Halfway Rock Light, or Sequin Light, just to name a few. You can custom design a trip to visit these iconic navigational aids. Pre-designed 3-hour tours start as low as $40.

Plan your sightseeing tours with Coast Guard licensed and insured Capt. Len Duda who has over 33 years of experience navigating Casco Bay, on-board a 25' 2018 Robalo center console charter boat.

See the Beautiful Coast, Wildlife, and Historic Sites View Halfway Rock Lighthouse, the WW2 Submarine Watch Towers, seals, porpoise, bald eagles, ocean sunfish, loons, osprey, cormorant nesting sites, the Cribstone Bridge, Mackerel Cove, Harriet Beecher Stowe's "The Pearl of Orrs Island", and all that the area has to offer.

Visit Admiral Robert Peary's Summer Home - Eagle Island Spend 2 hours in the museum and exploring the island trails. Search the beach for sea glass and marvel at the spectacular views. Enjoy a 30 minute scenic boat ride to and from the site. The Island is open from June 15 to Labor Day from 10-5.
